Zero Liability (FERPA Compliant)

Because Aumtech acts only as a proxy engine, we never persist, cache, or duplicate student records. The university has the ultimate kill-switch in their own Supabase dashboard.

Bring Your Own DB (BYOD)

Institutes simply provide their EdNex connection string. Aumtech scales to thousands of universities instantly without complex database migrations or data siloing.

Real-time Accuracy

A "sync" job runs the risk of data staleness (e.g., missed financial aid holds). A stateless proxy queries the source of truth in real-time on every page load.
