# aumtech.ai — Student Success Navigator
# Full Demo Script (7–10 Minutes)
# Target Audience: University Admins, Academic Leadership, Investors

---

## SCENE 01 — INTRO (45 seconds)
**[Screen: Login page — large aumtech.ai logo animating in]**

> "Meet aumtech.ai — the Student Success Navigator.
> 
> Today, universities face an enormous challenge. Thousands of students — each with a unique academic journey, financial situation, and wellness needs — and yet most institutions still rely on outdated email threads, siloed spreadsheets, and manual advising sessions to manage them all.
> 
> aumtech.ai changes that. It's a unified, AI-powered platform that transforms how students experience college — and how institutions serve them. From the moment a student logs in, they have a personal AI companion with them every step of the way.
> 
> Let's take a tour."

---

## SCENE 02 — SIGN IN & FIRST IMPRESSION (30 seconds)
**[Screen: Login — user types email and password, clicks Sign In]**

> "The sign-in experience is clean and professional. Students log in with their university credentials.
> 
> What happens next is immediate: our AI backend instantly loads their personalized profile — courses, GPA trends, outstanding holds, and a dynamically generated insight — all before the page even finishes rendering."

---

## SCENE 03 — DASHBOARD OVERVIEW (60 seconds)
**[Screen: Dashboard landing page with 'Good afternoon, Ram!' card]**

> "Welcome to the Student Dashboard. The very first thing a student sees is a warm, personalized greeting from the AI Navigator — noticing the time of day, their academic progress, and surfacing the most important action items right away.
> 
> In the center, we have the On-Track Score — a real-time metric that reflects GPA, assignment completion, holds status and wellness across all courses. Below that, Quick Actions give instant one-click access to the most common student needs: checking holds, booking an advisor, or talking to the AI.
> 
> On the left sidebar, the intelligent navigation panel organizes all features across five categories: Home, Academics, Resources, Campus Life, and Administrative. No hunting around. Everything is where you expect it."

---

## SCENE 04 — AI NAVIGATOR CHAT (90 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'AI Navigator' in sidebar → chat opens]**

> "At the heart of aumtech.ai is the AI Navigator — a conversational academic agent powered by Google Gemini.
> 
> This isn't a generic chatbot. It knows this student. It has access to their enrolled courses, GPA history, academic calendar, and more. Watch this.
> 
> I'll ask: 'I failed my Calculus midterm — what should I do?'
> 
> [pause — AI responds]
> 
> Incredible. In seconds, the AI analyzed the student's full academic context, identified Calculus II as a course where they are struggling, recommended booking a tutoring session, flagged an upcoming retake opportunity, and suggested the appropriate academic petition form — all in natural language.
> 
> Every conversation is saved in the chat history panel. Students can return to prior sessions, pick up exactly where they left off, and even export key advice. This is truly a next-generation academic advising experience, available 24/7, not just during office hours."

---

## SCENE 05 — COURSES & DEGREE ROADMAP (60 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'Courses' → Course list → then 'Degree Roadmap']**

> "Under Academics, students get a crystal-clear view of every course they are taking. Grades, credit hours, and AI-generated improvement suggestions appear automatically.
> 
> But we go further. Click on the Degree Roadmap — and now the student sees their entire academic journey laid out visually. Which credits are complete. Which are in progress. What's missing before they can graduate. No more mysterious advising holds about missing prerequisite courses. The AI Navigator proactively surfaces gaps before they become problems."

---

## SCENE 06 — TUTORING CENTER (90 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'Tutoring Center' → sync roster → book appointment]**

> "One of aumtech.ai's most powerful features is the intelligent Tutoring System.
> 
> Built on what we call 'Roster Truth' — the system syncs directly with the university's LMS, whether it's Canvas or Blackboard, to know exactly which courses a student is enrolled in.
> 
> Watch what happens when a student tries to book a session.
> 
> [Click sync roster]
> 
> Their full course schedule, synced live from the LMS. CS101, Calculus II, Academic Writing — all verified by the system.
> 
> Now they select Intro to Computer Science, enter a triage note — 'I am struggling with Python Lists' — and attach a screenshot of the error they are seeing.
> 
> [Click book]
> 
> In under two seconds, three things happen simultaneously. One: Our AI analyzes the triage note and generates a concise Brief for the Teaching Assistant. Two: Our intelligent Round Robin load balancer automatically assigns the TA with the lowest current workload. Three: A confirmation email is sent to the student.
> 
> This is not just scheduling. This is intelligent academic triage at scale — the kind of system that would typically take a team of human coordinators to run."

---

## SCENE 07 — WELLNESS CHECK & STUDY TIMER (45 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'Wellness' → quick survey → then click 'Study Timer']**

> "aumtech.ai doesn't just track grades — it cares about the whole student.
> 
> The Wellness Check-In is a brief, thoughtful survey that checks in on how the student is feeling. The responses are processed by the AI to personalize recommendations — whether that's a study break, a campus resource, or a gentle nudge toward counseling services.
> 
> And for students who need focus, the Study Timer provides a built-in Pomodoro-style productivity tool. Session data is even factored into the on-track score."

---

## SCENE 08 — HOLDS & ALERTS CENTER (45 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'Holds & Alerts' — show active hold cards]**

> "One of the biggest sources of student frustration is the mystery hold — a financial or administrative block that prevents registration, with zero explanation of how to resolve it.
> 
> aumtech.ai's Holds Center shows every active hold in clear, plain language: what it is, why it exists, how much is owed if applicable, and exactly what the student needs to do to resolve it. One click, and the student can initiate resolution — no more emailing three different offices to figure out why they can't register."

---

## SCENE 09 — FINANCIAL AID NEXUS & SCHOLARSHIPS (60 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'Financial Aid Nexus' → show scholarships → click AI Match]**

> "Financial stress is one of the top reasons students drop out. aumtech.ai tackles this head-on with the Financial Aid Nexus.
>
> The platform surfaces available scholarships tailored to the student's profile. But we don't stop there — click 'AI Match', and Gemini instantly analyzes the student's GPA, major, background, and interests against every available scholarship, ranking them by fit with an explanation.
> 
> Even more powerful: click 'Draft Statement', and the AI writes a personalized, professional scholarship essay — ready to review and submit. What used to take a student days of writing now takes seconds."

---

## SCENE 10 — SOCIAL CAMPUS (45 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'Social Campus' → show Study Groups and Marketplace]**

> "College isn't just academics — it's community. The Social Campus module connects students with each other.
> 
> The Study Buddy Finder matches students studying the same courses, so they can form groups and book study sessions together. The Peer Mentoring hub lets upperclassmen offer guidance to incoming students.
> 
> And the Textbook Marketplace? Students can buy and sell textbooks directly within the platform — no more third-party sites, no more awkward meetups."

---

## SCENE 11 — ADMIN PANEL & DEAN'S ANALYTICS (60 seconds)
**[Screen: Click 'Admin Panel' → Student Risk View → Campaign tools → Tutoring Analytics]**

> "Now let's flip to the other side of the platform — the view that university administrators and academic leadership see.
> 
> The Admin Panel gives leadership a live dashboard of all students, ranked by academic risk. High GPA students are flagged green. Students in trouble are flagged red, with detailed context.
> 
> From here, admins can launch targeted outreach campaigns in a single click. Select all high-risk students in a specific major, write a message, hit send — and the system logs every campaign with full audit trails.
> 
> And thanks to the Tutoring Analytics module, the Dean can see which courses have the highest demand for tutoring, which topics students are struggling with most, and how effectively the TA team is responding — all in real time."
