🔧 How to Resolve Your Hold

  1. Log in to the Student Portal and navigate to Billing & Payments.
  2. Review your outstanding balance breakdown (tuition, fees, etc.).
  3. Set up a Payment Plan or make a full payment via ACH/card.
  4. If a FAFSA or scholarship is pending, submit the Verification Checklist under Financial Aid.
  5. Allow 24–48 hours for the hold to automatically clear after payment.
  6. If it doesn't clear, contact the Financial Aid office directly via the AI chat below.

📌 Common Hold Types

  • Financial Hold — Unpaid tuition balance
  • FAFSA Hold — Missing financial aid verification docs
  • Academic Hold — Placed by advisor (requires advisor sign-off)
  • Library Hold — Overdue fines or unreturned items
  • Health Hold — Missing immunization records
