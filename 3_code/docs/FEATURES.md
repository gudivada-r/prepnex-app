## Core Dashboard
The **Dashboard** serves as the central hub for the student experience. 

### Features:
- **Welcome & Identity**: Displays a personalized welcome message with the student's name and an AI-generated daily insight.
- **Key Statistics**:
    - **GPA**: Real-time display of the student's current Grade Point Average.
    - **On-Track Score**: A percentage score indicating how well the student is progressing towards their degree requirements.
- **Quick Actions**: One-click access to high-frequency tools:
    - Holds & Alerts (Action Required)
    - Book Advisor (Schedule meetings)
    - Tutoring Center (Study help)
    - Drop/Add Forms (Administrative tasks)
    - Study Timer (Focus tools)
    - Career Finder (Internships/Jobs)
- **AI Support Team**: Direct access to specialized AI identities:
    - **The Tutor**: Course content specialist.
    - **The Admin**: Forms and deadlines expert.
    - **The Coach**: Wellness and support guide.
- **Sidebar Navigation**: A responsive sidebar providing access to all modules of the application, grouped by category (Home, Academics, My Records, Tools & Support, Campus Life).

## AI Navigator
The **AI Navigator** is a persistent, intelligent assistant powered by Google Gemini, designed to help students with various aspects of university life.

### Modes:
- **General/Navigator**: The default chat interface for general queries.
- **The Tutor**: Specialized in explaining academic concepts, reviewing essays, and helping with homework.
- **The Admin**: Focuses on university procedures, deadlines, and administrative paperwork.
- **The Coach**: Offers support for mental health, stress management, and campus social integration.
- **FAFSA Expert**: A specialized mode within the Financial Nexus assisting with FAFSA applications and financial aid questions.

### Capabilities:
- **Context Awareness**: The AI is aware of the student's profile, major, and current academic standing.
- **History**: Use the "My History" feature to review past conversations.

## Academics
Tools designed to help students manage their coursework and degree planning.

### Degree Roadmap
- **Drag-and-Drop Planning**: Visual interface to plan courses across upcoming semesters.
- **Audit Sidebar**: A list of required courses for the student's major that can be dragged into semesters.
- **AI Auto-Plan**: Automatically generates a recommended course schedule based on requirements and prerequisites.

### Courses
- **LMS Integration**: Sync courses, assignments, and grades from Canvas or Blackboard.
- **Course List**: Detailed view of currently enrolled courses.
- **Grades & Progress**: View current grades and progress within specific classes.

### Schedule / Book Advisor
- **Calendar View**: Weekly view of class schedules.
- **Advisor Booking**: System to schedule appointments with academic advisors.

### Syllabus Scanner
- **Upload & Analyze**: Upload course syllabi (PDF/Text) to automatically extract key dates, grading policies, and reading lists.
- **Calendar Integration**: Extracted dates can be synced to the student's calendar.

### Lecture Notes
- **Voice Recording**: Record lectures directly within the browser.
- **AI Transcription**: Automatically transcribe audio to text using Google Gemini models.
- **Summarization**: Generate concise summaries of lecture content.
- **Keyword Extraction**: Identify key terms and topics.
- **Flashcard Generation**: Create study flashcards directly from lecture notes.

## My Records
Administrative tools for managing student data and university forms.

### Drop/Add Forms
- **Digital Forms**: Submit requests to drop or add courses without paper forms.
- **Status Tracking**: Track the approval status of submitted requests.

### Progress
- **Academic Analytics**: Detailed breakdown of academic performance over time.
- **Credits Tracking**: Monitor earned credits vs. required credits for graduation.

### My History
- **Activity Log**: A timeline of student actions, AI interactions, and completed milestones.

## Tools & Support
Utilities to enhance student productivity and wellbeing.

### Study Timer
- **Pomodoro Technique**: Built-in timer with work/break intervals (e.g., 25/5 minutes).
- **Focus Stats**: Track total focus time and sessions completed.
- **Customization**: Persistent settings for timer durations.

### Flashcards (Smart Study)
- **AI Generation**: Generate flashcards from notes, syllabi, or topics.
- **Spaced Repetition**: Review interface to help memorize concepts effectively.

### Tutoring Center
- **Tutor Matching**: Find and book sessions with waiting tutors based on subject expertise.
- **Peer Tutoring**: Connect with other students offering tutoring services.

### Financial Nexus
- **Overview**: Dashboard summary of balance due, aid disbursed, and spending.
- **Scholarship Matcher**: AI-driven tool to find scholarships matching the student's profile and background.
- **Statement Creator**: AI-driven tool to draft personal statements for scholarships.
- **Tuition & Billing**: View transaction history, statements, and manage payments.
- **Grants & Loans**: Detailed view of accepted aid packages and disbursement status.
- **FAFSA Assistant**: Dedicated AI chat for FAFSA help.

### Career Pathfinder
- **Career Exploration & Skill Gap**: Analyze current skills vs target role requirements using AI.
- **Resume Generator**: Create AI-tailored resumes based on coursework and student profile.
- **Internship Matching**: Find relevant mock internship opportunities.

### Holds & Alerts
- **Alert Center**: Centralized view of all academic or administrative holds preventing registration.
- **Resolution Actions**: Direct links or instructions on how to resolve specific holds.

## Campus Life
Features to foster community and student well-being.

### Social Campus
- **Study Buddy Finder**: Match with classmates for study groups based on courses and study habits.
- **Peer Mentoring**: Connect upperclassmen mentors with freshman mentees.
- **Textbook Marketplace**: Buy, sell, or trade textbooks with other students.

### Wellness Check
- **Mental Health Check-in**: Daily or weekly mood tracking.
- **Resources**: Access to campus counseling and mental health resources.
- **Guided Exercises**: Breathing exercises and mindfulness tips.

## Faculty Portal
*Accessible only to users with Faculty permissions.*
- **Student Signals**: System for faculty to raise early warning signals (attendance, grades, behavior) for at-risk students.
- **Communication Drafts**: AI assistance to draft supportive emails to students.
- **Class Roster**: View lists of students in taught courses.

## Admin Panel

- **User Management**: Specialized tools to manage student and faculty accounts.
