# Student Success Navigator - Official Training Manual

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the Student Success Navigator! This platform is your all-in-one companion for university life, combining academic planning, financial aid tracking, career development, and AI-powered support.

**Target Audience:** Students, Faculty, and Administrators.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Logging In
1.  Navigate to the application URL.
2.  Enter your university email and password.
3.  Click **Login**.
4.  *First Time User?* Click **Sign Up**, enter your details, and verify your email.

### 2.2 Setting Up Your Profile
*Crucial for AI recommendations.*
1.  From the Dashboard sidebar, click **Settings** or the **Edit Icon** next to your stats.
2.  Update your **Major**, **GPA**, and **Interests** (e.g., "Machine Learning", "Renaissance Art").
3.  Fill in the **Background** section—this helps the AI find relevant scholarships for you.
4.  Click **Save Changes**.

---

## 3. Student Training Modules

### 3.1 Planning Your Degree
*Goal: Create a roadmap for graduation.*
1.  Click **Degree Roadmap** in the sidebar.
2.  **Drag and Drop**: Locate required courses in the "Audit" panel on the left and drag them into specific semesters (e.g., Fall 2025).
3.  **Use AI Auto-Plan**: If unsure, click the **AI Auto-Plan** button. The system will automatically place courses based on prerequisites and availability.
4.  **Verify**: Ensure no red warning icons appear (indicating prerequisite conflicts).

### 3.2 Using the AI Navigator
*Goal: Get instant answers effectively.*
1.  Click **AI Navigator** or **Chat**.
2.  Select a "Persona" based on your need:
    *   **The Tutor**: For homework help ("Explain derivatives").
    *   **The Admin**: For deadlines/forms ("When is the drop deadline?").
    *   **The Coach**: For stress/advice ("I feel overwhelmed").
3.  Type your question. You can reference uploaded documents (like a syllabus) if needed.

### 3.3 Managing Financial Aid
*Goal: secure funding and pay bills.*
1.  Navigate to **Financial Nexus**.
2.  **Check Overview**: View your "Total Balance Due" and "Pending Aid".
3.  **Scholarship Matcher**: 
    - Click the **Scholarships** tab.
    - Click **Match Me**. The AI will rank scholarships based on your profile.
    - Click **Draft Statement** to have the AI write a starting draft for your application essay.
4.  **FAFSA Assistant**: Use the "FAFSA Copilot" tab to chat specifically about tax forms and eligibility.

### 3.4 Study Tools
*Goal: Improve academic performance.*
1.  **Lecture Notes**: 
    - Go to **Lecture Notes**.
    - Click **Record** during class.
    - Afterward, view the **Auto-Summary** and **Flashcards** generated from the lecture.
2.  **Study Timer**: 
    - Go to **Study Timer**.
    - Select a mode (Focus, Short Break).
    - Start the timer to track productivity steps.

## 4. Faculty Training Modules

### 4.1 Raising Early Signals
*Goal: Identify at-risk students.*
1.  Log in with your Faculty credentials.
2.  Go to **Faculty Portal**.
3.  Locate a student in your **Class Roster**.
4.  Click **Raise Signal**.
5.  Select a category (Attendance, Grades, Wellbeing).
6.  **AI Auto-Draft**: Click this to generate a supportive email template to send to the student.

---

## 5. Admin Training Modules

### 5.1 System Configuration
1.  Log in as Admin.
2.  Go to **Admin Panel**.
3.  Manage user accounts (reset passwords, update roles).
4.  Configure global semester dates and deadliness.

---

## 6. Troubleshooting & FAQ

**Q: The AI isn't answering my specific question.**
*A: Ensure you are in the correct "Mode" (e.g., don't ask The Tutor about financial aid). Try rephrasing with more context.*

**Q: My course list is empty.**
*A: Go to "Courses" and click "Sync with LMS" to pull data from Canvas/Blackboard.*

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
*A: Use the "Help" icon in the bottom right or email support@university.edu.*
