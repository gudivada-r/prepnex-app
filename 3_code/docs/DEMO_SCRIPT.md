# Student Success Navigator - Demo Script

**Duration:** 15-20 minutes  
**Audience:** University administrators, faculty, or students  
**Presenter Preparation Time:** 10 minutes

---

## Pre-Demo Setup (5 minutes before presentation)

### Required Accounts
1. **Student Account:** `student@university.edu` / `student123`
2. **Faculty Account:** `faculty@university.edu` / `faculty123`
3. **Admin Account:** (if demonstrating admin features)

### Browser Setup
- Open application in Chrome/Edge
- Have 2-3 tabs ready for quick switching between user types
- Ensure stable internet connection for AI features

### Optional: Pre-load Data
- Add 3-4 sample courses to student account
- Create 1-2 holds/alerts for demonstration

---

## Demo Flow

### Part 1: Introduction & Dashboard (3 minutes)

**[Navigate to Login Page]**

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "Welcome to the Student Success Navigator - an all-in-one platform designed to help students navigate university life from enrollment through graduation."
- "This platform combines academic planning, financial aid, career development, and AI-powered support in one place."

**[Login as student@university.edu]**

**SHOW:**
- Personalized welcome message with student's name
- Key statistics: GPA (should show 3.5) and On-Track Score (85%)
- Quick action cards (Holds & Alerts, Book Advisor, Study Timer, etc.)

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "The dashboard provides a personalized view based on the student's profile."
- "Notice the AI-generated insight at the top - this updates based on academic performance."
- "Students get one-click access to commonly used tools."

**DEMO ACTION:** Click the edit icon next to stats, show profile settings modal briefly.

---

### Part 2: AI Navigator - The Core Intelligence (4 minutes)

**[Click "AI Navigator" from sidebar]**

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "The AI Navigator is powered by Google Gemini and acts as a 24/7 academic advisor."
- "We've designed three specialized personas to help with different needs."

**SHOW: The Three Personas**
- **The Tutor:** "Helps with homework, explains concepts, reviews essays"
- **The Admin:** "Answers questions about deadlines, forms, registration"
- **The Coach:** "Provides wellness support and stress management advice"

**DEMO ACTION:**
1. Click on "The Tutor" persona
2. Type: "Explain the mitochondria in simple terms"
3. Wait for response (should explain it's the powerhouse of the cell)

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "The AI maintains context throughout the conversation and can reference the student's courses and profile."
- "All conversations are saved in 'My History' for future reference."

---

### Part 3: Academic Planning - Degree Roadmap (3 minutes)

**[Navigate to "Degree Roadmap" from sidebar]**

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "The Degree Roadmap helps students visualize their path to graduation."
- "Students can see all required courses and plan them across semesters."

**DEMO ACTION:**
1. Show the audit panel on the left with required courses
2. **Drag** a course (e.g., "Introduction to Psychology") from the audit to "Fall 2025"
3. Click **"AI Auto-Plan"** button

**SHOW:**
- The AI automatically schedules remaining courses
- Prerequisite checking (if available, show a warning icon)

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "The AI considers prerequisites, course availability, and recommended sequencing."
- "This takes the guesswork out of registration planning."

---

### Part 4: Financial Nexus & Scholarship Matching (4 minutes)

**[Navigate to "Financial Nexus"]**

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "Financial aid is one of the biggest pain points for students. We've centralized everything here."

**SHOW Overview Tab:**
- Total balance due
- Aid disbursed year-to-date
- Next payment deadline

**[Click "Scholarship Matcher" tab]**

**DEMO ACTION:**
1. Click **"Match Me"** button
2. Wait for AI to analyze and show top matches (should show 2 scholarships with match scores)

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "The AI analyzes the student's GPA, major, background, and interests to find relevant scholarships."
- "Each match includes a reasoning for why it's a good fit."

**DEMO ACTION:**
3. Click **"Draft Statement"** on one of the scholarships
4. Show the AI-generated personal statement draft

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "Students can use this as a starting point and customize it further."

**[Click "FAFSA Assistant" tab]**

**SHOW:**
- Step-by-step FAFSA checklist
- Important deadlines
- **AI FAFSA Expert** chat button

**TALKING POINTS:**
- "FAFSA can be confusing. Students can chat with our specialized FAFSA Expert for questions about tax data, dependency status, etc."

## Common Demo Pitfalls & Solutions

**Issue:** AI takes too long to respond  
**Solution:** Pre-load a sample response or mention "In production, responses typically take 2-3 seconds"

**Issue:** No courses showing in Degree Roadmap  
**Solution:** Click "Sync with LMS" button first, or manually add sample courses before demo

**Issue:** Scholarship matcher shows "API Key Error"  
**Solution:** Explain that the demo AI is limited, show mock matches instead

---

## Key Messages to Emphasize

1. **All-in-One Platform:** "Students no longer need 5 different apps for registration, financial aid, tutoring, and planning."
2. **AI-Powered:** "Google Gemini integration provides personalized, 24/7 support."
3. **Saves Time:** "Features like AI Auto-Plan and Auto-Draft reduce hours of manual work."
4. **Evidence-Based:** "Early intervention signals help faculty support struggling students before it's too late."
